Lovely Greens Smoothie

Ingredients :

  • 1 cup Pineapple
  • 2 cups Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Grapes
  • 1 1/2 cup Orange Juice
  • 1 Banana
  • Ice

Recipie :

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • Peel and chop the pineapple if using fresh.
    • Peel the banana.
    • Rinse the spinach and grapes thoroughly.

  2. Blend the Smoothie:
    • In a blender, add the pineapple, spinach, grapes, orange juice, and banana.
    • Add a handful of ice cubes to make the smoothie cold and refreshing.

  3. Blend Until Smooth:
    • Blend all the ingredients on high speed until smooth and well combined. If the smoothie is too thick, you can add more orange juice to adjust the consistency.

  4. Serve:
    • Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy immediately.
